## 7 Step Savage Advertising System (from Notion Template):

### Step 1: Tell AI About Your Product / Story
- Input product details, brand story, unique angles

### Step 2: Find The Big & Easy Avatars
- Identify the most responsive audience segments
- "Big" = large market, "Easy" = low resistance to buying

### Step 3: Pick Your Avatar & Generate Ad Ideas
- Choose your target avatar
- Use AI to brainstorm ad concepts and angles

### Step 4: Start Creating Winning Scripts
- Write video ad scripts based on chosen angles
- Follow a batch production model (Script Batch 1-7, 8-12, 13-22)
- Multiple angles per product (e.g., Lions Mane: Brain Angle, Acne Angle)

### Step 5: Generate Shot Lists / Spokespeople
- Create detailed shot lists from scripts
- Identify and hire authority figures/spokespeople
- Track: Contracts Signed, Potential, Status

### Step 6: Hiring Editors: Sending Scripts and Files
- Outsource video editing
- Send scripts + raw footage to editors
- Track editing pipeline

### Step 7: Name & Organize Your Videos
- Proper naming convention for ad variations
- Organize by product, angle, batch, editor
