## THE 16 WORDS

> **"The secret to converting copy is to define the ONE BELIEF, then answer these TEN QUESTIONS."**

Simplified: **One Belief. Ten Questions.**

---

## THE ONE BELIEF (Commander's Intent)

Every sales letter must make the reader believe ONE thing:

> **"This new opportunity is the key to their desire, and it's only attainable through my new mechanism."**

### 3 Elements of The One Belief:
1. **New Opportunity** — What makes this UNIQUE (your USP). If it's not new, it's useless.
2. **Prospect's Desire** — What they want (financial security, freedom, health, status). Must be emotionally tied.
3. **New Mechanism** — HOW your solution works. The vehicle that delivers the new opportunity. WHY only yours can do it.

### One Belief Formula:
> "[New Opportunity] is the key to [Desire] and it's attainable only through [New Mechanism]."

### Examples:
- **P90X:** "Avoiding the plateau effect is the key to building muscle, attainable only through the P90-X 'muscle confusion' system."
- **Biz-op VSL:** "Getting in the trade BEFORE the news is the key to making easy money every week, attainable only through the patent-pending Weekly Paycheck Indicator."
- **Biz-op (generic):** "Plugging into existing demand [New Opp] is the key to quitting the 9-5 [Desire], attainable only through the Back-Door Income Method [New Mechanism]."

## THE 10 SEQUENTIAL QUESTIONS

### Q1 — How is this DIFFERENT from everything else I've seen?
**Purpose:** Inject dopamine. Novelty activates the substantia nigra (brain's "novelty center"). Without novelty = no dopamine = they stop reading.
**Copy action:** Open with what's NEW. USP goes in headline and first page. It must feel like a breakthrough discovery.
**Biz-op examples:** New AI system, new loophole, new model, new platform, new law, new conspiracy.

---

### Q2 — What's in it for ME?
**Purpose:** The most important word in any sales letter is "YOU." Answer this within seconds or they leave.
**Copy action:** Make the BIG PROMISE immediately. Tie it to their core desire.
**Formula:** "Make $[X]/month without [pain #1], [pain #2], or [pain #3]."
**Biz-op example:** "Make $10,000 a month online without building a website, hiring a copywriter, or even developing a product."
**Rule:** Answer Q1 AND Q2 within the FIRST PAGE.

---

### Q3 — How do I know this is REAL?
**Purpose:** Combat skepticism after the big promise. Proof = king.
**Copy action:** Show proof IMMEDIATELY but wrap it in ABT storytelling format (not boring data dumps).

**ABT Structure (And / But / Therefore):**
- **AND** = background/setup (builds momentum)
- **BUT** = conflict/tension (makes it interesting)
- **THEREFORE** = conclusion/result (closes the loop)

**Example:** "The company had 'sell' ratings AND earnings were declining. BUT then my indicator flashed a buy signal. THEREFORE, had you placed a simple trade, you could have made $9,500 that day."

**Proof types to use:**
- Historical charts/screenshots
- Third-party quotes (Forbes, Bloomberg, CNBC)
- Testimonials wrapped in ABT mini-story
- Live demos (open account on screen → show results)

---

### Q4 — What's HOLDING ME BACK?
**Purpose:** Justify their past failures. Release shame. Plant hope.
**Core insight (Blair Warren):** "Millions are looking for someone to take responsibility OFF their shoulders. Assuring others they are not responsible is essential for gaining influence."
**Copy action:** Reveal the REAL problem — the one thing all their previous attempts missed. This is the UMP (Unique Mechanism of the Problem).
**Formula:** Reverse-engineer Q1. If your USP is "get in BEFORE the news," then the real problem is "you've been getting in AFTER the news — by the time you act, it's too late."
**Biz-op version:** "Every system you tried sent you through the front door — the most competitive, most expensive entry point. That's why they all failed. It was never you."
**Key phrase:** "It's NOT your fault. Here's why..."

---

### Q5 — Who/What is to BLAME?
**Purpose:** Activate tribal instinct. "Us vs. Them" = oxytocin release = stronger group loyalty = more buyers.
**Copy action:** Identify and attack a common enemy. Must leverage EXISTING beliefs — never create an enemy from scratch.
**Biz-op enemies that work:**
- Wall Street insiders ("the game is rigged against the little guy")
- Tech billionaires hoarding AI advantages
- Corrupt politicians / the system
- Corporate employers who cap wages
- Gurus selling broken models

**Example copy:** "Remember Wall Street's Gordon Gecko: 'You're either on the inside or the outside.' Everyday investors like you have always been on the outside. That rigged game ends today."

---

### Q6 — Why NOW?
**Purpose:** Destroy inertia. Create urgency. The "either-or" false dilemma technique.
**The 4 resistance levels:** Reactance → Distrust → Scrutiny → **Inertia** (Q6 kills inertia)
**Copy action:** Raise the stakes with an "either-or" scenario. FOMO. Missed window. Closing opportunity.
**Hitler's technique (reframed):** Create a binary choice where inaction = losing everything. Either you act now, or the window closes and you miss the entire opportunity.
**Biz-op timing triggers:**
- "AI is consolidating — first movers capture 80% of the market"
- "We can only accept 10 new members this month"
- "The algorithm updates in 30 days — those already inside will benefit 10x more"
- "This free beta period ends when we go public at $25,000"

---

### Q7 — Why should I TRUST you?
**Purpose:** Establish credibility before asking for the sale.
**3 Storylines (choose one or mix):**

**A. "I've Been In Your Shoes"** (most powerful)
- You had the SAME problem, worse
- You discovered the mechanism through struggle
- Now you've achieved what they desire
- Makes reader think: "This guy gets it. He's just like me."

**B. Robin Hood**
- You worked inside the system (Wall Street, Silicon Valley, Big Pharma)
- You stumbled onto a secret they didn't want you to share
- You're blowing the whistle to level the playing field

**C. Expert**
- Credentials, media appearances, years of experience
- Used when you can't tell a personal struggle story

**Rule:** Answer Q1–Q7 in the first 1/3 of the sales letter. Emotional sale is done by the end of Q7.

---

### Q8 — HOW does it work?
**Purpose:** Help prospects justify their emotional decision with logic. Make them nod: "Yeah, that makes sense."
**Copy action:** Explain the mechanism clearly, logically, and wrapped in ABT story format.
**Key:** Channel an EXISTING BELIEF to support your mechanism. Dr. Brinkley used "goats are sexual dynamos" to sell goat testicle transplants. What does your avatar already believe that supports your mechanism?
**Rule:** The mechanism explanation is also COPY — it cannot be boring. Story format always.

---

### Q9 — How can I GET STARTED?
**Purpose:** Make a no-brainer offer. Create a massive GAP between perceived value and price.
**Key insight:** Never lower the price — it signals low quality. Instead, STACK VALUE.
**S.I.N. Offer formula (Todd Brown):** Superior + Irresistible + No-brainer

**Value Stack Template:**
1. Main product (reveal special report/program)
2. Introduce the subscription/access model
3. Testimonials for the service
4. False close (anchor a high price)
5. Stack bonuses with individual values
6. Show total value ($23,450 worth)
7. Reveal final price ($1,999)
8. Guarantee (full risk reversal)

**Biz-op offer framing:** "This is NOT a course, webinar, or ebook. This is a turnkey, done-for-you system."

---

### Q10 — What do I have to LOSE?
**Purpose:** Close with push-pull. Never be needy. People chase what moves away from them.
**The mistake:** Begging for the sale = 100% conversion killer
**The technique:** Present 3 options without recommendation. Let them choose. Create the illusion of control.

**3-Option Close Template:**
- Option 1: Do nothing (stay where you are — describe the pain of staying put)
- Option 2: DIY (try to do this yourself — describe how hard it is)
- Option 3: Take our offer (let us do the heavy lifting)

**Key line:** "Only you can decide." (Never beg. Move away. They'll chase.)

**Final paint:** "Two types of people in this world — those who dream about [desire] without acting, and those who take action when the opportunity presents itself. Since you've watched this far, I think you're one of the few special ones."

## KEY EVALDO PRINCIPLES TO REMEMBER

1. **Q1 + Q2 must be answered on page 1** — or they leave
2. **Proof must be wrapped in ABT storytelling** — never just data
3. **Q4 = The "It's not your fault" frame** — most emotional moment in the letter
4. **Q7 is done by the 1/3 mark** — emotional sale complete before mechanism reveal
5. **The offer section should never feel desperate** — push-pull = people chase what moves away
6. **The One Belief = 1 sentence written BEFORE the first word of copy**
7. **This framework works for any niche:** financial, health, biz-opp, supplements, relationships
